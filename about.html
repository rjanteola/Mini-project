About San Nicolas

San Nicolas, located in Ilocos Norte, is a vibrant town with a rich history and a strong sense of community. Known for its traditional pottery, heritage structures, and dynamic festivals, San Nicolas is a must-visit destination for those seeking culture and charm.

Home to the iconic Damili Festival and the enduring craft of damili pottery, the town is a testament to the Ilocano people's creativity and resilience. Come and experience the blend of history and innovation in this remarkable place.
